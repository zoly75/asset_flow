How can we help you?

Documentation, guides, and tips to manage your inventory efficiently.

1. Add Employees

List the people who will physically hold the assets (e.g., John Doe).

2. Create Assets

Add laptops, tools, or vehicles to the system and assign them to employees.

3. Print Labels

Stick QR codes on items. Scan them anytime to update status or return assets.

Documentation

It is important to understand the difference between these two lists:

  • Employees: These are the people who possess the equipment. They CANNOT log in to the software. They are just names in the database (e.g., "John Smith from Sales").
  • Team Access (Users): These are your colleagues (Admins, Warehouse Managers) who CAN log in to AssetFlow to manage the inventory. They have a username and password.

To add an asset, click the + Add New button on the Dashboard. You can fill in the Name, Serial Number, and Description.

You can assign the asset immediately or later. If you assign it to an Employee, the status automatically changes to Assigned / In Use. If you return it, it becomes Available.

Every asset gets a unique, secure QR code. You can:
  • Scan it with your phone: It will open the asset's public page.
  • Scan it while logged in: You can quickly update the status, re-assign it, or edit details.
  • Print Labels: Use the "Labels" button on the dashboard to generate a PDF sheet of QR codes for printing.

Only the Account Owner (Boss) can:
  • Manage Company Settings (Name, Phone).
  • Add or Remove Team Members (Login users).
  • Manage Billing & Subscription.
Team Members can manage assets and employees but cannot change company-wide settings.
